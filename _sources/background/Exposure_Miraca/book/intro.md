There are multiple ways to access information on critical infrastructure. For country-level assessments, Government and public agencies generally publish information about roads, railways or power network. These datasets often offer high accuracy, and include comprehensive attributes and consistent data. However, some information or specific infrastructure assets might have limited access.

Other sources of data come from international organisations and research institutes, that build on public data sources and, combined with additional sources of data, such as satellite imagery or models.
